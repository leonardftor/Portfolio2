About Me

My Introduction

With over 20 years of progressive experience in the financial services industry, I’ve led large-scale Agile and Waterfall initiatives across top-tier banks and credit card programs. My career has evolved from business analysis and application development into senior project and Scrum Master leadership, enabling me to bridge the gap between technology, business strategy, and delivery execution. I specialize in Scaled Agile (SAFe) environments, driving program governance, risk and dependency management, and forecasting to ensure consistent, high-quality outcomes. Over the years, I’ve successfully coached cross-functional teams and executives in Agile principles, championed continuous improvement, and acted as a change agent fostering innovation and delivery excellence. Recognized for my ability to lead diverse teams through complex transformations, I combine servant leadership, strong communication, and a deep understanding of Agile frameworks, Lean principles, and project governance to deliver solutions that align with organizational strategy and regulatory compliance. My mission is to build empowered, self-organizing teams that deliver measurable business value and long-term success.
